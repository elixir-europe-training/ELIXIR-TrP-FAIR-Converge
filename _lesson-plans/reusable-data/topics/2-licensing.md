---
title: License Statements and Reusability
status: draft
description: >-
  To be FAIR and reusable, published resources should always include clear
  license information by reference to an open published license. This makes it
  clear how the creator intended for it to be used, and so that other parties
  can reuse it knowing that they are not infringing the rights of the author.
